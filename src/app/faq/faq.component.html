DO YOU OFFER SLIDING SCALE?

If you are experiencing financial hardships, we do have limited slots offered on a sliding fee scale and is based on need and availability. We are happy to discuss your specific situation during your initial phone consultation.



DO YOU TAKE MY INSURANCE??

Paper Cranes Counseling is considered an out-of-network provider. What that means is that you will pay for therapy at the time of your session and seek potential reimbursement from your insurance. It’s important to look at your benefits if you will be seeking reimbursement, although reimbursement is not guaranteed and you may not receive the full amount.

Most people have higher deductibles, so unless you have a major surgery planned, you may not hit your deductible. If you have to pay 10% up to your deductible, that means that you’ll pay out of pocket for in-network and out-of-network, so it’s about whether you want to decide on the therapist that will be the best fit for your needs or have your insurance decide.

Lastly, it’s important to know that insurances require we give you (or your spouse/child) a mental health diagnosis. This is part of your medical and insurance record. If you feel uncomfortable with having a mental health diagnosis, which is another reason you may not want to use your insurance benefits.

We are happy to discuss your specific situation during your initial phone consultation.
