After many years as an entrepreneur and business executive, I decided to follow my calling to become a therapist. I don't only work in theory and methods. I merge my understanding of how people relate to each other, as well as how they manage their feelings and emotions, with real-world experiences that I have encountered as a marketing professional and team leader. I have spent many years discovering how people make decisions, adopt behaviors, learn, and communicate, which is why I find extreme satisfaction in helping people learn more about themselves. I have received a M.A. in Counseling Psychology, with a focus on Depth Psychology, from Pacifica Graduate Institute. Depth psychology pays close attention to how the unconscious mind influences how we experience life. In addition to working with individuals, couples, and families to investigate unconscious motivations and what drives them, I have taken a great interest in the problems that men face in society. I believe that many of these issues are ultimately the catalysts for much of the pain and suffering we are seeing around the world today. . ​ Currently I work under the license of, and am supervised by: -Justin Yaros, LMFT, Lic. #92508 -Patricia Taylor, PsyD., Lic. #14882, LCSW, Lic. #5928 at the Living Success Center ​
